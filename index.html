About Us

Where Every Moment Becomes Art

At ShokherSrity, we believe that wedding photography is more than just capturing images - it's about preserving emotions, telling stories, and creating heirlooms that will be treasured for generations.

Led by the talented duo Kowsik Saha and Dip Pal, our team brings together technical excellence and artistic vision. With over 5 years of experience and 100+ weddings captured, we understand the nuances of Bangladeshi wedding traditions while adding a contemporary, cinematic touch.
